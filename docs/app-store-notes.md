## Review Notes Draft

HörspielTracker is a private tracking app for audio drama episodes. User-created episodes, notes, ratings, moods, and backups remain on device unless the user manually exports a backup. The app can fetch public remote catalog JSON files over HTTPS to keep episode lists current without requiring an app update. The app is not affiliated with any audio drama publisher or trademark owner.
