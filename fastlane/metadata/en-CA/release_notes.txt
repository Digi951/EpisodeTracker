This version tidies up the episode list and makes your favorites easier to spot at a glance.

• Favorites in the number slot: Favorited episodes now show a colored circle with a small heart right at the episode number — instead of a separate icon at the row's edge.

• Calmer rows: The bookmark badge is gone from the list. You'll still find and change it reliably in an episode's detail view.

• Customizable Top Pick: The hero card in Statistics can now be shown, hidden, and reordered like every other section.

Enjoy your listening!
